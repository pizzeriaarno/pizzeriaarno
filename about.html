About Us

Italian Restaurant

Our Story

Pizzeria Arno welcomes you to drop by and join the locals in our fun and friendly atmosphere. Our goal is to offer great Italian food at a reasonable price while maintaining great quality and service.
You can enjoy a selection of appetizers, Pizza, Pasta, Burrito, fresh Salads, desserts and our daily specials, including everything from classic favorites to modern cuisine. We also offer a full bar at reasonable prices.
Come join us for Italian food on our patio or in our comfortable dining room. We are coocking Italian food since 2001.

Motaher H found himself in the world of food purely by chance when he started coocking Pizza at the age of 17. Over the years, different culinary styles were mixed, matched and combined to help invent what is Pizzeria Arno today.

Joyce Bowman Chef

His true passion was revealed when he started working at his family’s Pasta restaurant at the young age of 19. From there, Joyce continued to train at various restaurants. Today, he is one of the top inspirations for Italian cuisine.
